A Wakefield Tradition

Since 1990, Omelette Headquarters has been the go-to breakfast spot for locals and visitors alike. Our cozy restaurant is housed in a beautifully preserved train station on Water Street, bringing history and hospitality together.

We believe breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and we take that seriously. From our signature omelettes to our fluffy pancakes and crispy bacon, everything is prepared with care using quality ingredients.

Whether you're starting your day, enjoying a leisurely weekend brunch, or grabbing a quick lunch, we've got you covered with generous portions and friendly service that feels like home.
