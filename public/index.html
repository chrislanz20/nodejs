Our Story

Wakefield's favorite breakfast stop since the depot days.

Omelette Headquarters lives inside the town's former train station, keeping the original ticket windows, wood beams, and railroad photographs that celebrate Wakefield's history. Locals have gathered here for decades for huge portions, neighbors who remember your order, and coffee that never runs dry.

The Pitman family still greets guests at the door, plates up omelettes that overflow the dinner plate, and keeps the pace fast so you can get to work, school, or your weekend plans with a full stomach.
