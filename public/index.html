Our Story

A Wakefield breakfast tradition.

Omelette Headquarters has been serving Wakefield's favorite breakfasts for years from our historic train station home. Family-owned by the Pitman family, we're known for dinner-plate omelettes, friendly service, and coffee refills that keep your mug full.

The original depot ticket windows, wood beams, and railroad photos give the dining room a nostalgic feel that keeps regulars and first-time visitors coming back. Whether you're meeting friends before work, stopping in with family on the weekend, or grabbing takeout on the go, you'll find generous portions at an honest price.

This isn't fancy brunch—it's breakfast done right. Hot plates, quick smiles, and the hometown comfort Wakefield has loved for generations.
