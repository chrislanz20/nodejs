Our Story

The Wakefield café built on breakfast joy.

Omelette Headquarters began as a Lake Quannapowitt pop-up and grew into the town's beloved brunch clubhouse. We source from Middlesex County farms, churn our own cultured butter, and weave playful Boston energy into every plate.

Mornings start with espresso tastings, afternoons flow with mocktails, and Sundays are for neighborhood vinyl swaps. If it tastes like sunshine and feels like community, it belongs on our menu.

Chef Dana Avery

Founder & Executive Omelette Artist

Our Standards

Every omelette begins with uncompromising sourcing.

We contract with five New England farms to secure pasture-raised eggs, Vermont creamery dairy, and herbaceous produce. Our team whisks every omelette to order and cooks in carbon-steel pans seasoned daily.

  • Farm-to-pan: Weekly deliveries from Brookford Farm and Kimball Fruit Farm.
  • House-made sides: Preserves, pickles, and sourdough boules proofed in-house.
  • Community focus: 3% of brunch profits fund Wakefield school breakfast programs.
