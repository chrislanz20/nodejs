Deep Roots in the Melrose Community

Founded by F. Peter Waystack in 1994 and joined by partner Martina M. Kirby in the mid-2010s, Waystack & Kirby has remained a small, relationship-driven practice. With more than 70 years of combined experience, our attorneys offer attentive service and practical solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

We live, work, and volunteer in the communities we serve. From speaking at local elder law seminars to supporting neighborhood events, our firm is committed to being a trusted resource for Melrose and the surrounding North Shore.

Practice Areas

Comprehensive Planning for Families and Businesses

We guide clients through every stage of planning, protecting, and transferring their assets with clarity and care.

Estate Planning

Design wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and health care proxies that honor your wishes and secure your family’s future.

Elder Law & Medicaid Planning

Navigate long-term care options, MassHealth eligibility, and asset protection with compassionate guidance for seniors and caregivers.

Probate & Estate Administration

Settle estates efficiently, from court filings and inventories to distributions and trust administration.

Real Estate Transactions

Represent buyers, sellers, and lenders in residential and commercial closings, title reviews, refinancing, and property transfers.

Wills, Trusts & Powers of Attorney

Establish tailored instruments that empower loved ones to act when it matters most and ensure assets are passed on seamlessly.

Personal Taxation

Receive tax planning advice and IRS representation from an experienced, IRS-registered preparer serving clients along the East Coast.
