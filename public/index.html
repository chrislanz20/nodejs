Born from a Breakfast Obsession

Omelette Headquarters began as a pop-up on Wakefield’s lakefront farmer’s market and quickly became the town’s favorite morning ritual. Founder and chef Dana Avery wanted a place where guests could slow down, savor the moment, and experience eggs elevated beyond expectations.

Today, our kitchen celebrates the craft of breakfast with seasonal ingredients, a zero-waste mindset, and barista-crafted coffee drinks. Every omelette is folded to order, served with toasted sourdough, and paired with playful sauces you won’t find anywhere else.

Our Guiding Ingredients

  • Farm eggs sourced within 50 miles of Wakefield
  • House-fermented hot sauces and pickled vegetables
  • Compost-first kitchen with reusable packaging
  • Warm, neighborhood hospitality every day
