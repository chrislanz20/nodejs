Our Wakefield Story

The headquarters of happiness is breakfast.

Chef Dana Avery launched Omelette Headquarters as a lakeside pop-up and grew it into Wakefield's favorite brunch retreat. Every recipe begins with pasture-raised eggs, house-cultured dairy, and produce from friends at the Middlesex farmer’s market.

From sunrise espresso pours to late brunch mocktails, our team serves warmth, creativity, and a little bit of buttered theatre.

  • Seasonal menus that change monthly with local harvests
  • Playful plating inspired by artful Boston cafés
  • Community events supporting Wakefield creatives
