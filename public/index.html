Comfort Food with Community Roots

Eddy's Diner is proudly family-owned and operated, bringing heartwarming breakfasts and hearty lunches to Malden and Revere. We continue the legacy of the historic Linden Diner location that has served our neighborhood for more than 75 years.

From our famous Banana Foster French Toast to the fan-favorite Breakfast Bowl, every plate is crafted with care, using quality ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. Expect generous portions, friendly faces, and a welcoming atmosphere that treats every guest like family.
