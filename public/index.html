Not Just Coffee. A Community.

In 2021, Charbel Wehbe opened Union Cafe with one goal: create a true alternative to corporate coffee chains. After a decade of working in Lynn restaurants and saving every dollar, he replaced a Dunkin’ Donuts with something better.

Union Cafe is about real ingredients and real people. Real eggs in every breakfast sandwich. Desserts from our family bakery. Knowing your order by heart and greeting you with a smile every single morning.

We care about quality and service — not just making a quick buck. That’s why our staff goes home with generous tips, why the cafe is spotless, and why Charbel is here every day. You’ve tried the chains. Now try something real.
