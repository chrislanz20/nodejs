About Broken Yolk

Homestyle Cooking, Served with a Smile

We’re a family-owned breakfast diner rooted in Malden hospitality, sunflower décor, and generous plates.

Our Story

A Neighborhood Favorite Since Day One

Broken Yolk Restaurant opened its doors with a simple goal: serve the kind of hearty breakfast you’d expect from your favorite hometown diner. Years later, locals still line up for our made-to-order omelets, crispy home fries, and friendly service that remembers your name.

Inside, you’ll find red-and-white checkered tables, sunflower touches, and a cozy counter that feels like home. We’ve built a welcoming space for families, friends, and commuters to gather over the most important meal of the day.
