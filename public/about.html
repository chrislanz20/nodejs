Welcome to Kennedy's

Where local charm meets viral fame

Kennedy's Cafe began with Lisa Kennedy's dream to bring exceptional coffee, fresh food, and genuine hospitality to downtown Stoneham. Today our cafe is a viral sensation, but we still greet every guest like family.

Our Ice Latte Buckets have made headlines on Instagram and TikTok, but our heart is in our kitchen: hand-cracking eggs, toasting granola, and crafting drinks with premium ingredients. We believe in delivering wow-worthy experiences whether you dine in, grab and go, or order online.

Mission

Delight our community with craveable creations, welcoming service, and viral-worthy drinks while staying true to our Stoneham roots.

Meet the Owner

A lifelong Stoneham resident, Lisa opened Kennedy's Cafe to create a gathering space rooted in kindness, creativity, and high standards. You'll still find her behind the counter perfecting recipes and greeting regulars by name.
