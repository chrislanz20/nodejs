Our Story

Welcome to Eddy's Diner, a family-owned breakfast and lunch destination proudly continuing the legacy of the beloved Linden Diner. For more than 75 years, this corner of Beach Street has been a gathering place for neighbors, families, and friends — and we're honored to carry that tradition forward.

As a family, we believe in serving food that feels like home: generous portions, quality ingredients, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere from the moment you step inside. Whether you're craving our Banana Foster French Toast, a hearty Everyday Special, or the famous Breakfast Bowl, every dish is prepared with care and a whole lot of heart.

We're also proud to have team members like Gabby, whose excellent and speedy service keeps guests smiling and coming back week after week. When you're here, you're part of our diner family.
