Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Eddy's Diner values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information shared with us through our website or when you contact our team.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, phone number, and email when you reach out via form or phone.
  • Order preferences shared with our team for takeout, delivery, or reservations.
  • Anonymous analytics data about website usage (page views, device type).

How We Use Information

  • Responding to inquiries, reservations, and takeout orders.
  • Coordinating delivery and service through partner platforms.
  • Improving our website experience and menu offerings.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share necessary details with delivery partners (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub) to fulfill your order. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no online transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You may request updates or deletion of personal information by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of marketing messages by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us directly.
