A Breakfast Destination with Character

Step into The Omelette Headquarters and step back in time. Our family-owned diner combines classic American breakfast traditions with nostalgic railroad charm. From our signature three-egg omelettes to made-from-scratch Benedicts, every dish is crafted with care and served with a smile.

Our walls tell stories of the golden age of rail travel, with vintage Pennsylvania Railroad memorabilia transporting you to a simpler time. Whether you're a regular or first-time visitor, you're family here.
